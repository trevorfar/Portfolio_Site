Greatest Common Divisor Calculator

This project stemmed from me wanting to explore Discrete Mathematics and it's applications in coding.

It's a simple starter project, but I have plans to create an inverse calculator. The inverse calculator essentially works by back stepping through the steps of a GCD operation, so I thought it made sense to make this application first.

This application works by initializing a while loop that iterates until my input is equal to 0. I first find the remainder, or the modulus, of my two inputs by the moduluo function, then I set my first input, or my greatest, equal to my second, and my second equal to the found modulus. This essentially shifts the values to the left after each step, following conventional Euclidean algorithm architecture.
